Our Story

Where Every Stitch Tells a Story

Riwaayat — meaning "tradition" — was born from a college student's deep love for handcraft and the timeless art of crochet and embroidery. What started as a quiet hobby between study sessions blossomed into a small, heartfelt brand that celebrates the beauty of things made entirely by hand.

Every flower crocheted, every keychain woven, every bouquet assembled carries a piece of that love and intention. We believe handmade isn't just a style — it's a feeling. A warmth you can hold, a memory you can keep.
